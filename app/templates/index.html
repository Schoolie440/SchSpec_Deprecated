Who We Are

We are three brothers that spent our childhoods growing up together on a 33 acre patch of land that we knew as home. Between shooting cicadas with BB guns, carving trails through our woods for dirt bikes, and making ziplines in our barn, we always found that tinkering with stuff was a good way to spend our time. Today, the projects are a little bit bigger, but the passion for exploration and productivity is the same.

Currently, we are building this site to present some of our projects to the world. We hope to operate our own engineering consulting business someday so we figured that successfully running a website would be a good start. We are exploring the idea of becoming an LLC in the near future to further legitimize our business. For right now though, we are happy trying to give back a bit to the open source community that has given us so much. Check out the About Us page to find out more.

The Smart Switch

Finally, there's an easy way to tap in dotted eighth notes on your DL4. This small (1x1 in.) circuit will completely enhance the usability of your Line 6 DL4 unit. Based on the user friendly Arduino platform, the powerful ATtiny85 comes pre-programmed to give you 1/4 note to dotted 1/8th note conversion seemlessly. Beyond that, The Smart Switch gives you the ability to simulate an expression pedal input, giving you two presets per delay type, for a total of 6 presets without the need of a clunky, space consuming external expression pedal. Learn more about this mod here.
