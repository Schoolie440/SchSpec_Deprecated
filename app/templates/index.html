Featured Product: The Smart Switch

    Joe's latest project:

    The Smart Switch for the Line 6 DL4

    Finally, there's an easy way to tap in dotted eighth notes on your DL4. This small (1x1 in.) circuit will completely enhance the usability of your Line 6 DL4 unit. Based on the user friendly Arduino platform, the powerful ATtiny85 is comes pre-programmed to give you 1/4 note to dotted 1/8th note conversion seemlessly. Beyond that, The Smart Switch gives you the ability to simulate an expression pedal input, giving you two presets per delay type, for a total of 6 presets without the need of a clunky, space consuming external expression pedal.
